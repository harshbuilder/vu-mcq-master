How to Use

1.Select a subject from the top tabs on the Home screen.
2.Choose a Level and score 80%+ to unlock the next one.
3.Use Exam Mode for full 100-Q paper simulation with 15s timer.
4.Practice Previous Year Papers (2024 & 2025) for maximum accuracy.
5.Revisit the Mistake Bank daily to fix your weak areas.
🏆

Exam Strategy

✅Complete all levels of all 4 subjects first.
✅Solve 2025 Previous Year Papers — they are 90%+ accurate to real exam.
✅Do Exam 1 & Exam 2 mode 2–3 days before the exam.
✅Zero your Mistake Bank — revise until all are correct.
